As the supply of currency increases, it is understood that the spread will tighten. However, spread as tight as 0.038 USD is enough to make 100% profit a month in case you are trading reinvesting profit .

 

It is not always necessary to have the tightest spread to make money. It is important to evaluate the amount of reserves and daily trade volumes of that currency. Let's say that your spread is 0.06 and somebody else is trading at 0.04 with 1000 EUR of reserves. If daily trade volume is 10 000 EUR, it is not necessary to tighten your spread. The other trader’s 1000 EUR will be bought out soon and you will make money at your 0.06 spread.
